Aims and Objectves.
Open Source Academy is a Tech academy founded by a group of IT professionals based in Cairo, Egypt. Aims and Objectives of OSA are to positively contribute to the society by providing skills for boys and girls who have no means of attending schools; also to those who are in need and do not know where to start. OSA provides technical and online suport, training and many more.
